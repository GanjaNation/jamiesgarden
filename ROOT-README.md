## The Garden

Perched at 1,170 feet in the Santa Monica Mountains, Jamie's Garden is a high-elevation container garden combining cannabis, heirloom tomatoes, peppers, herbs, and companion flowers. Warm days, cool nights, exceptional airflow, and mountain light — conditions that push resin, terpenes, and flavor in everything we grow.

Every cultivar is chosen with intention. Genetic lineage is documented. Soil is living and reused season over season. Nothing is sold or distributed — this is personal cultivation as craft.

---

## System

- **Containers:** 25-gallon fabric pots
- **Soil:** Living soil — amended, inoculated, reused indefinitely
- **Water:** Dechlorinated · AACT applications throughout season
- **Pest management:** BT for caterpillars · Beneficial insects · No synthetic pesticides
- **Drying:** Whole-plant hang · Dedicated garage enclosure · Slow cure
